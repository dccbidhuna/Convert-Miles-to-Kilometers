About Us

Welcome to the Miles to Kilometers Converter. This tool is designed to help you quickly convert values between miles and kilometers or vice versa.

How to Convert

For converting miles to kilometers, use the formula:

1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers

To convert kilometers to miles, use the formula:

1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles

Examples:

  • 5 miles × 1.60934 = 8.0467 kilometers
  • 10 kilometers × 0.621371 = 6.21371 miles
